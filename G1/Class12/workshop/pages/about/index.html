Who Is the Hulk?

The Hulk is one of Marvel’s most powerful heroes. Born from gamma radiation, Bruce Banner transforms into a massive green warrior whenever anger takes over.

Mission

This site exists to celebrate Hulk’s story, power, and inspiration across movies, comics, and fan culture.

History

Created in 1962, Hulk has become one of Marvel’s most iconic heroes, known for strength and emotional depth.
