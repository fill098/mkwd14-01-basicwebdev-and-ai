## üéØ Introduction Purpose
This introduction to programming introduces the world of **web development**, basic programming principles, and the workflow of building and maintaining web applications.  
It explains the difference between **front-end**, **back-end**, and **full-stack** development and helps students decide their learning path. 
You will start to develop expertise in **prompt engineering**, strengthen your **critical thinking with AI**, and learn how to effectively **integrate AI tools** into the development lifecycle.

## üß≠ Introduction Structure
The course is divided into multiple sessions that cover:
- The basics of programming and logical thinking  
- Pseudocode and algorithms  
- Understanding front-end and back-end technologies 
- Introduction to AI-assisted programming and learning tools 
- Practical tasks
- Practicing logical thinking, brainstorming, and problem-solving with AI support 

Each session includes short theory, examples, and hands-on practice.
